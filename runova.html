What RuNova can do

Pick a mode below the chat to steer it.
Chat
General reasoning and conversation with rolling summaries, so long threads stay coherent without ballooning cost.
Document
Upload a PDF, DOCX, PPTX, CSV, Markdown, or HTML file. RuNova retrieves relevant passages, cites them, and summarizes on request. It won't invent what isn't there.
Calculate
A math engine for exact arithmetic, powers, roots, logs, and trig, with no floating-point guesswork.
Web search
Live results for current events and fresh facts, pulled in only when a question needs them.
Stock prices & trades
Live stock prices for any share on demand, plus simulated purchases that pause for your explicit approval before anything executes(demo purpose to shown HITL execution).
Weather & time
Real-time conditions for any city and current date/time in any timezone.
Memory
Durable facts about you are recalled across conversations, deduped, and injected only when relevant.
Safety
Guardrails on every input and output: prompt-injection blocking, PII redaction, and a groundedness check that flags possible hallucinations.
